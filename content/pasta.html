Pasta!

By hand

Made: 5/22/23 (success, dough a little dry and tough to work with, tasted good though)

  • Flour
  • Eggs
  • Salt
  • Olive oil (optional)
  • Water (optional)
  1. Make a mound of flour with a hole in the middle and add the other ingredients
  2. Beat the egg with a fork while slowly incorporating the flour, forming a dough
    • Mash with fork and form the dough around the fork, picking up all the flour
    • OR don't worry about excess dough and transition into folding flour over the forming dough
  3. Knead
  4. Wrap tightly and let rest for at least 30 minutes
  5. Cut into smaller pieces (if necessary)
  6. Roll out and laminate (fold into thirds, rotate, continue rolling) at least three times
  7. Roll out one final time until slightly less than opaque, then fold/roll up and slice to desired thickness

"Carbonara"

Made: 5/22/23 (success, perhaps less bacon next time)

  • Cheese (parmesan and/or romano)
  • Eggs and extra egg yolks
  • Black pepper
  • Bacon
  • Garlic
  1. Whisk eggs and cheese together and add black pepper
  2. Slice bacon and cook over medium heat
  3. Strain out some of the fat
  4. Add garlic and briefly saute over medium low
  5. Add cooked pasta and some pasta water
  6. Add egg/cheese mixture and toss
